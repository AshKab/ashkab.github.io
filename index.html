About Sowmya

Art that honours

Sowmya is a certified freelance makeup artist based in Salem and Erode, bringing elegance and precision to every function — from intimate mehndi ceremonies to grand wedding receptions.

Known for fresh, natural, and classy looks that celebrate each bride's unique beauty, Sowmya uses only genuine luxury products and travels to your venue for a relaxed, personal experience.
